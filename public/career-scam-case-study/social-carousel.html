SOCIAL CAROUSEL DECK

Use these modules as 4:5 social posts or workshop slides. Each panel isolates one concept for fast comprehension.

Slide 01

Unexpected recruiter text?

Pause immediately. Unsolicited job outreach is a common scam entry point.

Slide 02

Text-only interview

If all hiring communication stays in text/chat, risk rises sharply.

Slide 03

Pay sounds too good

Large compensation for simple remote tasks is a lure mechanism.

Slide 04

“We’ll send a check”

The equipment-check pivot is a known fake-check scam path.

Slide 05

What scammers want

Trust first. Then identity details, then money movement.

Slide 06

Best response

Stop, verify independently, and escalate reporting if already exposed.
