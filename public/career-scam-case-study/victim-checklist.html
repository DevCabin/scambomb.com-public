VICTIM CHECKLIST

Use this checklist if someone may have already replied to a fake recruiter or shared information, money, or documents.

1
Stop contact immediately

Do not keep replying by text, email, or chat.

2
Save the evidence

Keep screenshots, email addresses, phone numbers, check images, receipts, and messages.

3
Contact the bank or payment provider

If money moved or a check was deposited, call right away and explain it may be fraud.

4
Protect personal information

If you shared your Social Security number, ID, date of birth, or bank details, treat it as a possible identity theft risk.

5
Report the scam

Report it to the FTC, IC3, the job platform, and the real employer being impersonated.
