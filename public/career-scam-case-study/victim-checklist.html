VICTIM CHECKLIST

Use this when someone may have already engaged with a fake recruiter scam.

1
Stop contact immediately

Do not continue text/email exchange.

2
Preserve evidence

Save screenshots, addresses, phone numbers, check images, and receipts.

3
Contact bank/payment provider

If money moved or a check was deposited, call right away.

4
Treat shared personal data as risk

If SSN, ID, DOB, or bank details were shared, assume identity-risk event.

5
Report

FTC, IC3, job platform, and impersonated employer.
