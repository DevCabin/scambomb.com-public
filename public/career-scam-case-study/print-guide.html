CAREER SCAM PRINT GUIDE

A one-page workshop handout for fake recruiter and job text scams.

Top warning signs

  • Unsolicited recruiter contact from unknown channel
  • Interview process kept entirely in text/chat
  • Unusually high pay for simple remote tasks
  • “We’ll mail a check for equipment/supplies”

Immediate action steps

  • Stop contact and save all evidence
  • Contact bank/payment provider if money moved
  • Treat shared personal info as identity-risk
  • Report to FTC, IC3, platform, and impersonated company
