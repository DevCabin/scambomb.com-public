CAREER SCAM ALERT

Fake recruiter messages are increasingly polished. Here’s the quick pattern to watch for.

Three-pattern warning

Unexpected outreach + text-only interview + check-for-equipment request is a high-risk combination. Encourage immediate pause-and-verify behavior before any data or money movement.

What to tell readers

Stop contact, preserve evidence, call bank if funds moved, and file reports through official channels.
