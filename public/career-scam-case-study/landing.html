THE FAKE RECRUITER TEXT SCAM THAT CATCHES ACTIVE JOB SEEKERS

This shareable awareness page explains the scam in plain language, highlights the biggest warning signs, and gives clear next steps before anyone loses money or personal information.

What to do now Read full case study

Fake trust

Scammers often use the name of a real company to sound believable right away.

Rushed process

A text-only interview and an unusually fast offer are major warning signs.

Equipment check scam

If they say they will send money or a check for supplies, treat it as a serious red flag.

What is at risk

In one scam, a person can lose money and also expose important personal information.

Best defense

Pause, look up the company on your own, and verify through official contact information.

If someone already responded

Save the messages, contact the bank right away if money moved, and report the scam through official channels.
