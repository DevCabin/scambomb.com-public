🟢 7. Scheduling Posts Guide

How to schedule a Facebook post to publish at a later date and time

✅ Goal of this task

You are going to complete this task slowly, carefully, and one step at a time. There is no rush.

Step 1

Open Facebook

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Click the address bar.
  3. Type facebook.com.
  4. Press Enter.
👀 What you should see:The Facebook homepage.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you see a login screen, log in.
  • If nothing loads, check internet.
Step 2

Go to the ScamBomb Page

  1. Look on the left side.
  2. Click Pages or Your Pages.
  3. Find ScamBomb.
  4. Click ScamBomb.
👀 What you should see:The ScamBomb Facebook page.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see Pages, click the menu with 3 lines.
  • If ScamBomb is missing, check your account.
Step 3

Start a New Post

  1. Find What’s on your mind? or Create post.
  2. Click inside the box.
👀 What you should see:A pop-up window and blinking cursor.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • Click again inside the box.
  • Refresh the page if needed.
Step 4

Create Your Post

  1. Type your message.
  2. Add an image or video if needed.
  3. Click Photo/Video to choose a file.
👀 What you should see:Your text and image or video, if added.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • Fix text by clicking and editing.
  • Remove a wrong image with the X.
Step 5

Find the Scheduling Option

  1. Look at the bottom right of the post window.
  2. Find the blue Post or Publish button.
  3. Look carefully for a small arrow beside it.
  4. Click the small arrow, not the main Post button.
👀 What you should see:A small menu opens.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If it posts immediately, you clicked the main button by accident.
  • Next time, click the small arrow.
Step 6

Click Schedule

  1. In the menu, click Schedule.
👀 What you should see:A new window opens with date and time options.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see Schedule, look carefully in the menu.
  • If the menu disappears, click the arrow again.
Step 7

Choose Date and Time

  1. Click the date field.
  2. Pick the day you want the post to go live.
  3. Click the time field.
  4. Choose the time.
👀 What you should see:Your selected date and time appear on the screen.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If the time looks confusing, pick a simple time like 9:00 AM.
  • If you click the wrong time, change it again.
Step 8

Confirm the Schedule

  1. Click the Schedule button in that window.
👀 What you should see:The window closes or returns you to the post screen.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click Schedule again.
Step 9

Final Schedule Button

  1. Look at the bottom right again.
  2. The button should now say Schedule.
  3. Click Schedule.
👀 What you should see:The window closes and your post is saved as scheduled.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If it posts immediately, scheduling was not set.
  • Try again and watch for the word Schedule.
Step 10

Check Scheduled Posts

  1. On your page, look for Professional dashboard or Meta Business Suite.
  2. Click it.
  3. Look for Scheduled posts.
👀 What you should see:Your scheduled post listed with the date and time.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, it may not have scheduled correctly.
  • Try scheduling again.

🎉 Done!

You completed this task. Take your time every time. You are doing great.
