🟢 4. Canva Video Guide

How to open a Canva video template, replace the text slides, and export it as a video (MP4)

✅ Goal of this task

You are going to complete this task slowly, carefully, and one step at a time. There is no rush.

Step 1

Open Canva

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Click the address bar.
  3. Type canva.com.
  4. Press Enter.
👀 What you should see:The Canva dashboard with your designs.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you see a login page, log in.
  • If nothing loads, check your internet.
Step 2

Open Your Video Template

  1. Click Projects or Your designs.
  2. Find your video template.
  3. It may say Scam Video Template.
  4. Click it.
👀 What you should see:A video editing screen with a large preview and smaller slides.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, check Recent designs.
  • If nothing opens, click again.
Step 3

Understand the Slides

  1. Look at the bottom of the screen.
  2. You will see small boxes. These are slides or pages.
  3. Each slide is one part of the video.
👀 What you should see:Multiple slides in order.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see slides, look on the left side instead.
Step 4

Edit Text on Slide 1

  1. Click Slide 1.
  2. Move your mouse over the text.
  3. Click directly on the text.
  4. Type your new message.
👀 What you should see:The text updates as you type.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click directly on the words again.
  • If text disappears, click Undo.
Step 5

Edit Text on the Next Slides

  1. Click Slide 2.
  2. Click the text.
  3. Type the new message.
  4. Repeat for each slide.
👀 What you should see:Each slide updates with your new text.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you skip a slide, go back and click it.
  • Go slide by slide slowly.
Step 6

Preview the Video

  1. Look near the top of the screen.
  2. Find the Play button. It looks like a triangle.
  3. Click Play.
👀 What you should see:The video plays from start to finish.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If it does not play, click again.
  • If slides go fast, that may be normal.
Step 7

Review Everything

  1. Watch the video once.
  2. Check for spelling mistakes.
  3. Make sure slides are in the right order.
👀 What you should see:A clean, simple video.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • Click any slide that needs fixing.
  • Preview again after fixing.
Step 8

Download the Video

  1. Look at the top right corner.
  2. Click Share.
  3. Click Download.
  4. Choose MP4 Video as the file type.
  5. Click Download.
👀 What you should see:The video begins processing and then downloads.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If download is slow, wait. Video downloads take time.
  • If it fails, try again.
Step 9

Find Your Video

  1. Open your Downloads folder.
  2. Look for the newest file.
  3. The file should end in .mp4.
👀 What you should see:Your finished video file.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, sort by Most Recent.
  • Try downloading again if needed.

🎉 Done!

You completed this task. Take your time every time. You are doing great.
