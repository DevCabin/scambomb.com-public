🟢 1. Facebook Posting Guide

How to create a Facebook post with text and an image, then publish now or schedule for later

✅ Goal of this task

You are going to complete this task slowly, carefully, and one step at a time. There is no rush.

Step 1

Open Facebook

  1. Open your web browser. This could be Chrome, Safari, or Edge.
  2. Click the address bar at the top of the screen.
  3. Type: facebook.com
  4. Press the Enter key on your keyboard.
👀 What you should see:The Facebook homepage. If you are logged in, you will see your feed. If not, you will see a login screen.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you see a login page, enter the email and password.
  • If the page will not load, check the internet connection.
Step 2

Go to the ScamBomb Page

  1. Look at the left side of the screen.
  2. Find and click Pages or Your Pages.
  3. Find ScamBomb in the list.
  4. Click ScamBomb.
👀 What you should see:The ScamBomb Facebook Page. The page name should be at the top.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see Pages, look for the menu icon with 3 lines.
  • If you do not see ScamBomb, make sure you are logged into the correct account.
Step 3

Start a New Post

  1. Near the top of the page, look for a box that says What’s on your mind? or Create post.
  2. Click inside that box.
👀 What you should see:A pop-up window or larger post box. You should see a blinking cursor.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click again directly in the text area.
  • If still nothing happens, refresh the page and try again.
Step 4

Write Your Text

  1. Start typing your message.
  2. Example: Scammers are getting smarter every day. Stay alert and always double-check before clicking links.
  3. You can press Enter to create space between lines.
👀 What you should see:Your words appearing in the post box.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you make a mistake, use the Backspace key.
  • If text disappears, click back into the box and keep typing.
Step 5

Add an Image

  1. Look below the text box.
  2. Click Photo/Video.
  3. When the file window opens, find your image. It is usually in Downloads or Pictures.
  4. Click the image once.
  5. Click Open.
👀 What you should see:The image appears inside your post as a preview.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see your image, check the correct folder.
  • If you picked the wrong image, click the small X on the image to remove it.
Step 6

Review Your Post

  1. Read your text slowly.
  2. Look at your image.
  3. Make sure everything looks correct.
👀 What you should see:Your full post with text and image together.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If something looks wrong, click back into the text or remove and re-add the image.
Step 7

Publish Now or Schedule for Later

  1. Look at the bottom-right corner for the blue Post or Publish button.
  2. If you want your post to go live now, click Post or Publish.
  3. If you want your post to go live later, click the small arrow next to Post or Publish, then click Schedule.
👀 What you should see:Either your post goes live now, or the Schedule setup window opens.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If it posts immediately but you meant to schedule, you clicked the main Post button.
  • Next time, click the small arrow and choose Schedule.
Step 8

If Scheduling: Set Date and Time

  1. In the Schedule window, click the date field and choose the day you want your post to go live.
  2. Click the time field and choose the time.
  3. When it looks right, click Schedule to confirm.
👀 What you should see:Your chosen date and time are saved for the post.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you picked the wrong time, change it before confirming.
  • If the window closes too soon, click the arrow by Post/Publish and choose Schedule again.
Step 9

Final Confirm Button

  1. If you already clicked Post or Publish in Step 7, your post is done and now live.
  2. If you are scheduling, make sure the final button says Schedule, then click Schedule.
👀 What you should see:If published now, your post appears on the ScamBomb page. If scheduled, your post is saved for later.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click the final Post/Publish or Schedule button one more time.
  • If Facebook freezes, refresh the page and try again.
Step 10

If Scheduling: Check It Was Saved

  1. On your Facebook Page, open Professional dashboard or Meta Business Suite.
  2. Look for Scheduled posts.
  3. Find your post in the list with the date and time you selected.
👀 What you should see:Your scheduled post listed with the correct date and time.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, it may not have saved correctly.
  • Go back and schedule it again slowly.

🎉 Done!

You completed this task. Take your time every time. You are doing great.
