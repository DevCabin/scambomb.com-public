🟢 2. Facebook Video Post Guide

How to upload and publish a video post on Facebook

✅ Goal of this task

You are going to complete this task slowly, carefully, and one step at a time. There is no rush.

Step 1

Open Facebook

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Click the address bar at the top.
  3. Type facebook.com.
  4. Press Enter.
👀 What you should see:The Facebook homepage and your feed.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you see a login page, log in.
  • If nothing loads, check your internet.
Step 2

Go to the ScamBomb Page

  1. Look on the left side.
  2. Click Pages or Your Pages.
  3. Find ScamBomb.
  4. Click ScamBomb.
👀 What you should see:The ScamBomb Facebook page.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If Pages is missing, click the menu icon with 3 lines.
  • If ScamBomb is missing, check the account.
Step 3

Start a New Post

  1. Find What’s on your mind? or Create post.
  2. Click inside the box.
👀 What you should see:A pop-up window and blinking cursor.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • Click again inside the text area.
  • Refresh the page if needed.
Step 4

Upload Your Video

  1. Look below the text area.
  2. Click Photo/Video.
  3. Find your video file. It is usually in Downloads or Videos.
  4. Click the video once.
  5. Click Open.
👀 What you should see:The video begins uploading. You may see a loading bar or spinning icon.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see your video, check the correct folder.
  • If upload fails, try again.
  • If it is slow, wait. Video uploads can take time.
Step 5

Wait for Upload to Finish

  1. Watch for the upload to complete.
  2. Do not click Post yet.
  3. Do not close the window.
👀 What you should see:The loading bar disappears and the video preview looks ready.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If it seems stuck, wait another minute.
  • If still stuck, cancel and try again.
Step 6

Add Your Text Message

  1. Click into the text box above the video.
  2. Type your message.
  3. Example: This scam is spreading fast. Watch this and protect yourself.
👀 What you should see:Your text appears above the video.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If text does not appear, click inside the box again.
  • If you make a mistake, use Backspace.
Step 7

Review Everything

  1. Look at your video.
  2. Read your text.
  3. Make sure everything looks right.
👀 What you should see:Text and video together in the post.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • Wrong video? Click the X on the video to remove it.
  • Text mistake? Click and fix it.
Step 8

Publish the Video Post

  1. Find the blue Post button.
  2. Click Post.
👀 What you should see:The window closes and your video post appears on the ScamBomb page.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click Post again.
  • If video is missing, upload again.

🎉 Done!

You completed this task. Take your time every time. You are doing great.
