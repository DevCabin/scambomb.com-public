🟢 6. YouTube Upload Guide

How to upload a short video to YouTube, add a title and description, and publish it

✅ Goal of this task

You are going to complete this task slowly, carefully, and one step at a time. There is no rush.

Step 1

Open YouTube

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Click the address bar.
  3. Type youtube.com.
  4. Press Enter.
👀 What you should see:The YouTube homepage.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you see Sign In, click it and log in.
  • If the page does not load, check your internet.
Step 2

Start Uploading

  1. Look at the top right corner.
  2. Find the camera icon with a plus sign.
  3. Click it.
  4. Click Upload video.
👀 What you should see:A screen that says Upload videos and a button that says Select files.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see the camera icon, make sure you are logged in.
  • If nothing happens, click again.
Step 3

Select Your Video File

  1. Click Select files.
  2. Find your video, usually in Downloads or Videos.
  3. Click the video once.
  4. Click Open.
👀 What you should see:The video starts uploading with a progress bar.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see your file, check the correct folder.
  • If upload fails, try again.
Step 4

Add a Title

  1. Look for the Title box.
  2. Click inside it.
  3. Type a simple title. Example: Watch Out for This Phone Scam.
👀 What you should see:Your title appears in the box.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you cannot type, click inside the box again.
  • Use Backspace to fix mistakes.
Step 5

Add a Description

  1. Look for the Description box.
  2. Click inside it.
  3. Type a short description.
👀 What you should see:Your description appears in the box.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing types, click again.
  • Fix mistakes with Backspace.
Step 6

Choose Not Made for Kids

  1. Scroll down slowly.
  2. Find Is this video made for kids?
  3. Select No, it’s not made for kids.
👀 What you should see:The No option is selected.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, scroll more.
  • This step is required.
Step 7

Click Next Three Times

  1. Look at the bottom right.
  2. Click Next.
  3. Click Next again.
  4. Click Next one more time.
👀 What you should see:You reach the Visibility screen.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If Next is greyed out, check that title and required fields are complete.
Step 8

Choose Public

  1. On the Visibility screen, find Private, Unlisted, and Public.
  2. Click Public.
👀 What you should see:The Public option is selected.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If you do not see it, look carefully on the screen.
  • If unsure, ask for help before publishing.
Step 9

Publish the Video

  1. Look for the Publish button.
  2. Click Publish.
👀 What you should see:A confirmation screen says your video is live.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If nothing happens, click again.
  • If it freezes, wait a moment.
Step 10

Check the Video

  1. You may see a link to your video.
  2. Click it to view the video.
👀 What you should see:Your video playing on YouTube.
⚠️ If something goes wrong:
  • If the video is not ready, wait a few minutes. Processing is normal.

🎉 Done!

You completed this task. Take your time every time. You are doing great.
