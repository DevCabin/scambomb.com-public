Method Notes: How to Read This
  • There is no unified global registry that tags layoffs as AI vs. non-AI.
  • These values should be interpreted as conservative lower-bound estimates.
  • “AI-attributed” here means AI/automation was explicitly cited by employer statements or reputable reporting.
  • Many firms likely use AI as a factor without naming it publicly in layoff rationale.
  • Trend: AI-linked attribution is rising.
