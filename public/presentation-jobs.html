Read This Carefully: What the Numbers Mean

  • These figures are a lower bound, not a complete count.
  • Many employers do not explicitly label cuts as “AI-driven.”
  • “AI-attributed” here means AI/automation was named or clearly cited as a central factor.
  • Data blends U.S. and selected global examples for directional insight.
  • Trend signal: AI-related layoff attribution is increasing, but still not yet the majority driver.
