The Uncomfortable Truth
🧠
AI doesn't think.
It's If-This-Then-That at hyperspeed. Understanding that makes you better at using it.
📊
The data matters more than the prompt.
Garbage in, garbage out. The best prompts can't fix a bad data source.
⚠️
Superusers become obsolete.
Prompt engineers are like SEO experts — valuable today, automated tomorrow.
✅
The durable skill is judgment.
Knowing when to trust AI, when to question it, and when to override it entirely.
