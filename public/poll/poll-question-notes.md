## 2. Trivia: Neighbor Spoofing

Question:
What percent of scam calls use a fake number that looks local — even though the caller is not?

Options:
- 50%
- 70%
- 90%

Correct answer:
- 70%

Presenter framing:
- A few years ago, researchers found that nearly 70% of scam calls in the US used neighborhood spoofing.
- Frame it as a past finding, not a current live statistic.

Source:
- First Orion, 2018 Scam Trends Report, summarized here:
- https://www.comparitech.com/blog/information-security/phone-spam-statistics/

## 3. Trivia: Caller ID Trust

Question:
True or False — If your caller ID shows your bank's real name, it is safe to trust the call.

Options:
- True
- False

Correct answer:
- False

Presenter framing:
- Caller ID can be spoofed to impersonate a trusted company or agency.

Source:
- FCC, Caller ID Spoofing:
- https://www.fcc.gov/consumers/guides/spoofing
